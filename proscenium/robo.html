Robo War

Description

Are you confident enough to code simple programs without looking at your monitor screen?
Then You Are At The Right Place !
Blind coding is a C/C++ programming event where competitors need to write the program by switching off monitors.

Rules and Rounds

  1. Team participation.
  2. Two members are allowed per team.
  3. 3 minutes of time with screen ON will be given to the team at the time of their choice.
  4. No specific branch restriction.
  5. Participants should report half an hour before the event.
  6. The decision of the Event Heads will be final and no correspondence will be entertained in this regard.
  7. Any group found cheating or not following the guidelines will be debarred immediately.
  8. Any kind of argument with the event members may lead to disqualification.
