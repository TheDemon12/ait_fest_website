RAMPAGE

Battle Of Bands
RAMPAGE - Battle Of Bands

Description

"Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything." These are the words used by plato to describe the activity that's could express your emotions without saying anything.
Conversance-cultural fest of AIACT&R presents you the RAMPAGE. The evening to the symphony created by bands of diverse genre will set the mood of every attendee.

Rules

  1. Students who are registered in any program in an educational institute of India, are currently studying in it and have a valid identity card can participate in the event.
  2. A band must contain minimum 3 members and maximum 8 members. Cross college teams are also allowed.
  3. No recorded material or loops are allowed.
  4. Acts of vandalism during performance would lead to straight disqualification.
  5. Drums, Mics and speakers will be provided, other than that, all equipment must be brought by the individual teams according to their needs (including leads/jack)
  6. The time limit is 3+8, i.e. 3 minutes for setup and 8 minutes for the performance.
  7. The decision of the judges and the organizing team will be final and binding.
  8. In case of cross college team, mention the college name of each participant.

8 Miles - Rap Battle

Description

You know it's funny when it rains it pours They got money for wars, but can't feed the poor.
For every dark night, there's a brighter day.
Reality is wrong. Dreams are for real. - 2PAC

The opportunity to showcase your talent is here, the most awaited event, PROCON 2020 proudly presents "8 MILES", the rap battle competition.
So come, join us as we drop the bass and be the part of the celebration.

Rules

  1. Do not bite (copy) someone’s works. Your freestyle must be 100% original material and shall not infringe upon any third party copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights nor shall it contain any vulgar words and sexually explicit lyrics.
  2. The language and dialect to be used in the battle are either English or Hindi.
  3. The rap lyrics (punch line) should focus more on humor whether you mock or ridicule your opponent.
  4. For the preliminary round of competition, each rapper will be given two minutes in which they showcase their prewritten/freestyle rap, for the battle round each rapper rhymes twice for two minutes thirty seconds per round. In case of a tie, another round (OT) will be provided to break the tie.
  5. Each Emcee must rhyme over the same beat as their opponent.
  6. Competitors who are rude, abusive or deemed otherwise unsuitable by the organizers will be disqualified from the competition.
  7. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
