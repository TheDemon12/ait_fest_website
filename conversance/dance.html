Exuberance

Group Dance
Exuberance - Group Dance

Description

Dance has been an example of how diverse we are throughout the world. It has been an important part of our Indian Culture. Dancers don't need wings to fly.
Well, the stage is set for all of you to dance freely but in a team at PROCON-2020. So, put your talent to test.

Rules and Rounds

  1. Students who are registered in any program in an educational institute of India and have a valid Identity card can participate in the event.
  2. Time limit is 5+3 minutes
  3. Each team should have minimum of 8 and maximum of 25 members, including coordinators (Stage limit is 15 persons at a time).
  4. Choice of songs is open to the participants.
  5. No props will be provided by the coordinators. Participants can carry their own props.
  6. The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization, expressions, and costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.
  7. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Danza - Duet Dance

Description

“Nobody cares if you can’t dance well. Just get up and dance.” Dance is the language of soul. PROCON-2020, the annual techno-cultural fest of AIACTR invites you to DUET dance competition.
So, get your troop and slay the audience with moves that don’t lie!

Rules and Rounds

  1. The maximum time limit for the performance - 4+1 minutes.
  2. Participants will be judged on the basis of choreography, creativity, originality ,skills, expressions and use of props.
  3. Participants are requested to bring the track in pen-drive. They are requested to carry a backup track.
  4. Participants must carry their props, costumes, etc.
  5. Lighting of matchsticks, cigarettes any other derogatory tasks are strictly not allowed on stage.
  6. Any sort of vulgarity, obscenity or hurtful actions will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Students who are registered in any program in an educational institute of India and have a valid Identity card can participate in the event.
  8. The decision of the judges and the organizing team will be final and binding.
    9. Note : Pairs of Girl + Girl and Boy + Boy are also allowed.
