MANTHAN - Poetry

Description

Slam poetry, a form of performance poetry that combines the elements of performance, writing, competition, and audience participation. The competition is open in several languages including Hindi, English, Urdu and others. It is an opportunity to get creative and let your imagination run wild. Come on the stage of MANTHAN at PROCON-2020 and live the moment.

Rules and Rounds

  1. Each Registrant have to submit their poetry in text format during registration and this will be used in shortlisting process. Out of which top 20 Registrant will be selected and will get a chance to perform at PROCON-2020.
  2. The time limit for the performance will be 3+1 minutes.
  3. No copied content will be allowed (the participant will be disqualified in case of plagiarism.)

JUST A MINUTE
JUST A MINUTE

Description

Just-A-Minute (or JAM) is an all-round fun event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth. Can you make it through sixty seconds of non-stop talking without hesitation, repetition or deviation? Or will the sheer pressure make you crumble and have your competitors pounce on you in an instant?

Rules and Rounds

  1. The decision of judges will be final and binding.
  2. You will get judged on any of the following counts:
    • Content of your speech
    • You stutter, stammer, and generally indulge in “time-wasting tactics”. These include efforts to buy time like interspersing your talk with “uhh”, “aah” etc.
    • Early start and late start
    • Speaking too fast or too slow
    • Grammatical errors
    • Undue stress on words, undue emphasis in the sentences
    • Repetition of a previously used idea
    • Not following the “random rules” as set by the JAM Head.
  3. You will get 1 minute to prepare for the speech after your topic is given.
  4. The total speaking time is sixty seconds, inclusive of all the time that the speaker is allowed to speak for.
  5. Points are given in the following manner:
    • Positive points for every second
    • Positive points for every correct point
    • Negative points for every incorrect point
    • Negative points for every second that you speak after sixty seconds
    • Points based on section 2.
  6. The winner is the person with the maximum number of points at the end of the round.
  7. Only college students with valid College I-cards in possession will be allowed to participate.
  8. While making payment, in the message section, please mention the following details :
    • Team Name
    • Event Name
