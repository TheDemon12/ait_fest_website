    RACHNA

    Face Painting Competition
    RACHNA - Face Painting Competition

    Description

    We laugh, we scream, we cry, we dream. Our face becomes a canvas of emotions. Ever thought of painting it with an imaginative creation of yours?
    Face painting is the artistic application of cosmetic paint to a person face. The event involve participation of two participants. The event is going to be a great fun for the participants as well as the spectators. Through this event we are intended to promote different field of arts other than the native one. Here's a chance to participate in RACHNA and showcasing your talent.

    Rules

    1. Only college students with valid College I-cards in possession will be allowed to participate.
    2. Teams should have two members only.
    3. Time duration is 50 minutes.
    4. All teams should use only cosmetic colors.
    5. Teams have to bring their own material required for the event.
    6. Judgement will be based on creativity, beauty, painting skills and final presentation.
    7. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    8. While making payment, in the message section, please mention the following details :
      • Team Name
      • Event Name
