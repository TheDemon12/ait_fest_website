About Procon

Proscenium (our technical fest) and Conversance (our cultural fest) coalesce together and give rise to PROCON. Conversance and Proscenium are the annual cultural and technical extravaganza celebrated in the month of March. Students from various colleges participate in this boisterous gathering of talent and knowledge.With a footfall of more than 8000, there is always a flow of energy and an aura of positively the moment one enters the campus. It is a good platform to develop inter college relations and promote new brands or product. This time it is going to be even bigger and better, with an expected footfall of around 10,000 people. AIACT&R invites everyone to be a part of this technocultural blast.
